Welcome to Rustige Oord

Where peace and tranquillity find you





Welcome to Rustige Oord - where peace and tranquillity find you. Nestled between Outeniqua Mountain and the Indian Ocean, the perfect spot to provide you with a peaceful and tranquil experience. Founded in 1998, it served as a wedding venue for a couple in need a venue. The guests were delighted by its beautiful views and atmosphere the home gave off - many of them said that they wanted to return. The home received so many compliments and attention that the owner decided to turn it into a Bred and Breakfast, to share her beautiful home with many others. People as well as tourist came from near and far to visit the place. There was a need to extend the home in the 2008 into the Guest House as you see it today.

The home consists of 4 beautiful, modern bedrooms - each comes with its own unique feel and each bedroom with its own complete bathroom. The bedrooms as well as the bathrooms are all newly renovated to suit the modern look. We have 2 living room areas, dining space and a stunning kitchen in the centre of the home. The pool area overlooks the cliff. Each of the 4 bedrooms has a balcony and / or patio that overlooks the beautiful garden. The home has uncapped fibre, all rooms are airconditioned and we are fully solar equipped to ensure that you are never left hot or cold as well as to make your stay as convenient as possible. Further to this, we offer laundry facilities at an additional charge, a shuttle that runs thrice a day to surrounding shopping centres, golf course and Airport, complimentary with your stay. Visit our amazing Yoga Studio, for early morning yoga and meditation and witness the breathtaking view. Book an appointment at our Spar, for a relaxing experience or enjoy the walk just after sunrise following the hiking trail whilst enjoying the wonderful scenery.

We are 3.5 km away from the Golf Course and 12,5 km away from George International Airport. We have shops and restaurants 5 km away.
